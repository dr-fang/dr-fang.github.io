About

Dr. Bin FANG is an Associate Professor in the Department of Management Science at the School of Management, Xiamen University. Prior to joining Xiamen University in 2016, he recieved his Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering (Information Systems major) from School of Management, Harbin Institute of Technology. His current research interests mainly focus on users' behaviour in the IT context, e.g., Generative AI, blockchain, social media, and platform economics.
